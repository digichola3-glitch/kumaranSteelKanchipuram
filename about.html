                                                  About Sri Kumaran Steels

                                                  Trusted wholesale supplier of steel & cement in Kanchipuram since 2012 — built on honesty, quality, and reliable service.

                                                  Our Story

                                                  Building Trust Since 2012

                                                  Sri Kumaran Steels, Kanchipuram was established in 2012 with a simple vision—to supply quality construction materials with honesty and reliability. What began as a small retail outlet has today grown into one of the trusted wholesale suppliers of steel and cement in the Kanchipuram region, thanks to the continued support of customers, builders, and contractors.

                                                  Over the years, the company has expanded its operations with dedicated departments including Marketing, Accounts, Administration, and Transport, ensuring smooth business operations and professional service to customers.

                                                  With a fleet of 15 delivery vehicles, Sri Kumaran Steels is able to deliver construction materials efficiently and on time to project sites. The company is supported by more than 50 committed employees, who play a vital role in managing operations, logistics, and customer service.

                                                  The company also maintains a strong network of over 500 masons and bar benders, and 50+ builders who regularly procure steel and cement, along with 200+ sub-dealers who purchase materials in wholesale quantities.
